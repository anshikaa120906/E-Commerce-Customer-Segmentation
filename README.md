**E-Commerce Customer Segmentation Using Machine Learning**

### Project Overview
This project applies data analysis and RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) modeling to segment 4,338 e-commerce customers into distinct business groups. This enables targeted marketing strategies designed to increase sales and customer retention.

### Customer Breakdown & Strategy
* **Standard Active (2,501 Customers):** Our largest steady group. Strategy: Standard promotional offers and item recommendations.
* **Loyal Customers (977 Customers):** Frequent shoppers with stable spend. Strategy: Premium VIP rewards and early access sales.
* **Churned/Inactive (778 Customers):** Lost shoppers. Strategy: Automated "We Miss You" win-back discount emails.
* **High-Value At Risk (82 Customers):** Large spenders who stopped buying. Strategy: High-priority, direct customer care interventions.

### Tech Stack
* **Python (Jupyter Notebook):** Data processing, feature engineering, and machine learning calculations.
* **Microsoft Excel:** Pivot tables, data analysis, and dashboard visualization.
